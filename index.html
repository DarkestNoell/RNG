Mythic Robot Quiz!

Everyone has a favorite robot or mechanical marvel.

Yours might be the unstoppable Terminator or the droll droid C3PO. Or maybe you prefer the lifelike hosts of Westworld, or the transmogrifying car-truck-plane Transformers.

But did you know, deep inside your favorite robot is some DNA from the ancient world?

Long before the robots of today, and well before modern SciFi, an amazing variety of mechanical creations, lifelike automata, and other man-created technologies were thought up by the ancients.

We know this because they wove them into stories, and painted, carved and cast them into artworks, and those things LAST.
